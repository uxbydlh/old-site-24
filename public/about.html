About

Hey, I'm Dorian, a UX/UI designer from Scotland.

I started my career as a software engineer following my education at the University of Strathclyde. The passion I had for accessible design never left me though, so I moved into the design field, where I could have a direct impact on that sort of thing.

Right now, I'm really enjoying web design. I believe that we should have fun and interesting places to visit online outside of the main, big social medias.

If you have an interesting idea that needs a designer's touch, I would love to hear from you. Drop me an email at: UXbyDLH@gmail.com
